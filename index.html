About Me

Where artistry meets strategy

I'm an artist whose nonlinear career path has taken me from professional stages to healthcare communications, building a unique skillset rooted in creative expression. My theatrical training cultivated my sense of timing, presence, and connection with audiences—skills that translate seamlessly into all of my professional work.

Working administratively in theatre honed my writing abilities and design sensibilities, while teaching me the value of collaboration over the long-term. I developed a sense of vision that balances immediate needs with broader mission, as well as the flexibility to shift focus as productions changed every few months.

My MSW deepened my understanding of human experience and interpersonal dynamics, coinciding with my exploration of AI tools that expanded what I could create independently. Now at Third Horizon, I've found a way to synthesize these seemingly disparate experiences into a cohesive professional identity that delivers exceptional value through creativity, empathy, and technical precision.

I bring to every project:

  • The artist's eye for aesthetic detail and emotional resonance
  • The actor's awareness of audience needs and expectations
  • The writer's clarity and persuasive power
  • The designer's ability to organize information visually
  • The social worker's understanding of human behavior and communication
