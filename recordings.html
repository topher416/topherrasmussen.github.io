Recordings

Music that exists — from bedroom loops recorded at 2 AM to full ensemble performances at Old Town School of Folk Music.

This isn't a polished discography. It's a record of sound that actually exists somewhere: three Radiohead ensemble sessions captured live, nine lo-fi bedroom recordings from 2015-2016 that never quite became songs, and a practice tool I built for myself that turned into something other people use. The music is uneven because I am uneven. Some of it is good. Some of it is loops that loop too long.

Practice

The tool I built for learning these songs.

FM Noggin

A practice guide for learning Radiohead covers with isolated stems, sheet music, and tempo-adjusted tracks. Powered by a custom Python pipeline that uses Demucs for AI source separation — feed it a YouTube link and it spits out individual instruments at whatever speed you need.
